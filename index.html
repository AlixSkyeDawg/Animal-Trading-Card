Displacer Beast

Creature - Monstrosity

These carnivorous cats are also known as dirlagrauns or omlarcats.

  • Size: Large
  • Type: Monstrosity
  • Challenge Rating: 3
  • Alignment: Lawful Evil

Displacer beasts are large cat-like creatures with six legs and a pair of tentacles 5 feet long sprouting from their shoulders. They're covered in blue-black fur and are usually about 9 feet long. Displacer beasts are not just hunters and predators, they're also killers. They enjoy the pusuit and kill for sport.
